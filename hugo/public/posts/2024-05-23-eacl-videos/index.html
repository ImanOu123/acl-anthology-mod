EACL 2024 videos

May 23, 2024

Recorded videos for EACL main conference and workshop papers are now available in the ACL Anthology.

We have checked the released videos against the permission forms that were signed for Underline and believe everything to be in order. If your video was inadvertently released against your wishes, please let us know by filing an issue on Github or by emailing the Anthology director and assistant.
